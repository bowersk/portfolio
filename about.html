About me

Mechanical Engineer specializing in Mechatronics

Hi, I'm Kenji Bowers and I'm a builder. My favorite thing is to think of something awesome, design it, and then go on to manufacture it; all of it. I started with purely mechanical things like Raptor, and my SpeakerWeb but I quickly realized that nowadays everything has a battery, microcontroller and motors controling it so I started building robots. I feel truely blessed to be living at this time where robots are finally breaking out of their cages and I am so excited to bring my passion, experience, and ideas to whatever I work on.

I'm currently the most interested in the intersection of robotics, agriculture, computer vision and the internet of things which is why I've spent the last few months after graduating in December on my WaterBot project, but I'm also interested in transportation, small scale manufacturing, and home automation. Please take a look at my Resume to see my professional experience and feel free to reach out!
